Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how VaultDigitals ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with our digital products and any internal tools we operate.

1. Information We Collect From Customers

When you purchase a digital product from us through Etsy, Etsy collects information necessary to process your transaction (such as your name, billing details, and email). Etsy operates under Etsy's Privacy Policy, which governs that data. We see only the order details Etsy makes available to sellers (the buyer name and any messages you send us).

We do not operate our own customer login system, and we do not collect customer information directly outside of Etsy.

2. How We Use Order Information

Order information from Etsy is used only to fulfill your order, respond to your questions, and meet our legal obligations as a seller. We do not sell, rent, or share customer information with third parties for marketing.

3. Internal Tools and Third-Party Integrations

We operate internal automation tools that interact with third-party platforms — including but not limited to TikTok, Etsy, and Google AI Studio — to manage our own brand accounts and content. These tools:

  • Use credentials issued to our business accounts only.
  • Are not exposed to customers or used to handle customer data.
  • Do not access, collect, or store data belonging to any users other than our own brand accounts.

When we authenticate with a third-party platform (such as TikTok via OAuth), the tokens and credentials issued to us are stored securely on our internal systems and used only to take actions on the corresponding brand account.

4. Cookies and Analytics

This legal information site does not use cookies or analytics. Any customer-facing storefronts we operate may use cookies and analytics tools governed by the platform hosting them (e.g., Etsy).

5. Children's Privacy

Our products and services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

6. Data Retention

Order information received from Etsy is retained as long as needed for fulfillment, customer service, and to meet legal/tax obligations. Authentication tokens for internal tools are retained only as long as needed for those tools to function and are rotated regularly.

7. Your Rights

If you are a customer who has purchased from us through Etsy and you wish to access, correct, or delete information we hold about you, please contact us at ai@quebiclabs.com. For information held by Etsy directly, contact Etsy through their help center.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date above will reflect the most recent revision.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at ai@quebiclabs.com.
