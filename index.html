What's inside the logbook.

Everything a heart failure caregiver needs to track — in one printable.

180 daily tracking pages

One page per day: weight, BP morning & evening, heart rate, SpO2, edema score, fluid & sodium intake, activity, daily notes.

AHA Zone Guide (built in)

Green / yellow / red traffic light system printed right in the book. Every morning, find your zone, take the right action.

Sodium reference table

Sodium content for 50+ common foods — soups, breads, cheeses, deli meats, restaurant staples. No more guessing.

26-week medication tracker

Log every dose, every change, every refill. Catches missed doses before they catch up with your loved one.

12 doctor visit pages

Pre-formatted "What to Tell My Cardiologist" prompt list so nothing gets forgotten at appointments.

Weekly summary pages

Spot trends at a glance. Bring meaningful data to the medical team instead of vague descriptions.
